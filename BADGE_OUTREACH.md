# Badge program — outreach email

Send from a personal address, one club at a time (or mail-merged with the fields
below). **Wave 1 (start here): `BADGE_OUTREACH_WAVE1.csv`** — 42 sendable clubs
whose listings we just enriched, each with a personalized {hook} column and a
{page_url}. Cadence: 20–30/day. The full long tail lives in
`club-outreach-list.csv` for later waves.

---

## Wave-1 template (warm — we just updated their page)

**Subject:** Just updated {club}'s page on Padel Courts Finder

Hi {contact_name or "there"} —

I run Padel Courts Finder, the directory of every padel club in the US
(331 clubs and counting). Quick heads-up: I just {hook} on your page —
players in {city} use it to find your courts and how to book:

{page_url}

   https://www.padelcourtsfinder.com/badge

That's it — no catch, listings are free. If you ever want priority placement
(homepage + top of your city page), that exists too, but the above costs nothing.

Come play,

## Notes

- The badge embed links from the club's site to their club page = a relevant
  backlink per club that installs it. Never mention SEO/backlinks in the email —
  the pitch is player visibility, which is also true.
- The "reply and I'll fix it" line doubles as the data-enrichment engine:
  corrections come to you with zero research.
- Follow-up (1 week later, only if no reply): one line — "Quick nudge on this —
  want me to double-check your listing details while I have it open?"
- Track sends/replies in the CSV. Clubs that install the badge are the warm list
  for featured-listing outreach later.
- Consulting off-sell: for engaged repliers (like Mia at Montauk YC / Jess at
  Nicol), add the soft bridge paragraph — "getting your program in front of the
  right players is the other side of what I do." Only after they reply, never
  in the first email.
- Wave-1 gotchas baked into the CSV: ids 240/244 (Xcel) share one inbox — send
  once covering both; ids 155/405 (10by20) same — one email to Manu covers both
  locations; 6 clubs have no published email (status `no_email`) — reachable by
  phone/IG DM or contact form if you want them, listed in the notes column.
