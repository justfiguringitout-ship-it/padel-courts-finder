# Badge program — outreach email

Send from a personal address, one club at a time (or mail-merged with the fields
below). Recipients: `club-outreach-list.csv`. Personalize {club}, {city}, {slug}.

---

**Subject:** Your club's page on Padel Courts Finder (+ a badge for your site)

Hi —

I run Padel Courts Finder, the directory of every padel club in the US
(312+ clubs and counting). {club} has a page on it — players in {city} use it to
find your hours, courts, and how to book:

https://www.padelcourtsfinder.com/courts/{slug}

Two quick things:

1. **If anything on that page is off** — court count, pricing, hours, photos —
   reply and I'll fix it same-day. It's your listing; I want it right.

2. **We made a badge for listed clubs.** Free, takes 2 minutes to add to your
   site's footer, and links players straight to your page:
   https://www.padelcourtsfinder.com/badge

That's it — no catch, listings are free. If you ever want priority placement
(homepage + top of your city page), that exists too, but the above costs nothing.

Come play,
Dito
Padel Courts Finder
https://www.padelcourtsfinder.com

---

## Notes

- The badge embed links from the club's site to their club page = a relevant
  backlink per club that installs it. Never mention SEO/backlinks in the email —
  the pitch is player visibility, which is also true.
- The "reply and I'll fix it" line doubles as the data-enrichment engine:
  corrections come to you with zero research.
- Follow-up (1 week later, only if no reply): one line — "Quick nudge on this —
  want me to double-check your listing details while I have it open?"
- Track sends/replies in the CSV. Clubs that install the badge are the warm list
  for featured-listing outreach later.
